Sweet and Sour Chicken

Cook time: 1:20

Servings: 6 to 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lbs. frying chicken
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup salad oil, hot
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 can (13 oz.) pineapple chunks (optional)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chicken drippings
  • 1 large green pepper, cut in strips
  • water

Recipe

  • Cut up 2-3lbs. frying chicken.
  • Coat chicken with 1/2 cup flour.
  • Brown chicken in 1/3 cup salad oil.
  • Place in shallow roasting pan, skin side up.
  • Sprinkle with 1 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper.
  • Drain and reserve juice from pineapple chunks can.
  • Add water to pineapple juice to make 1 1/4 cup liquid
  • In saucepan combine 1 cup sugar, 4 tablespoons flour, 1 1/4 cup pineapple juice plus water mixture, 3/4 cup vinegar, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1/4 teaspoon ginger (optional), 2 tablespoons chicken drippings.
  • Bring mixture to boil, stirring constantly. Boil 2 minutes. Pour over chicken.
  • Bake uncovered for 30 minutes at 350 degrees,
  • Add pineapple chunks (optional) and 1 large green pepper cut into strips.
  • Bake 30 minutes longer.
